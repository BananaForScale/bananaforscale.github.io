How to install the B&B version of TPPI

  1. Download the FTB Launcher into an empty folde
  2. Run the FTB Launcher. Click the PACK CODES button in the top right.
  3. Enter MegaSharkPunch as the code.
  4. Scroll down the available list of packs until you see TPPI
  5. Download and Launch TPPI version 1.0.4
  6. Close Minecraft
  7. Download the Extra Mods + Configs
  8. Unzip and copy the mods and configs to your /TPPI/minecraft/mods and /TPPI/minecraft/config folders (FTB created these folders where you saved and ran the FTB Launcher from)
  9. Run FTB Launcher again, start TPPI
  10. Connect to tppi.bocklebee.com
